Q1. In which situations does the list of numbers make an AP?
(i) Taxi fare: ₹ 15 for first km, ₹ 8 for additional km.
Fare for 1st km = 15
Fare for 2nd km = 15 + 8 = 23
Fare for 3rd km = 23 + 8 = 31
List: 15, 23, 31, ...
Difference = 23 - 15 = 8
Difference = 31 - 23 = 8
Difference is same.
Answer: Yes
(ii) Air in cylinder: Removes 14 of remaining air.
Initial air = 1
Removed 1st time = 14
Remaining = 1 - 14 = 34

Removed 2nd time = 14 × 34 = 316
Remaining = 34 - 316
= 12 - 316 = 916

d₁ = 34 - 1 = -14
d₂ = 916 - 34 = -316
Difference is not same.
Answer: No
(iii) Well digging cost: ₹ 150 first metre, rises by ₹ 50.
1st metre = 150
2nd metre = 150 + 50 = 200
3rd metre = 200 + 50 = 250
Difference = 50 (constant).
Answer: Yes
(iv) Compound interest: ₹ 10000 at 8% per annum.
Amount = P(1 + R100)ⁿ
Calculating amounts for each year.
Interest is on increasing amount.
So difference keeps increasing.
Difference is not same.
Answer: No

Q12. Two APs have same common difference. Diff between 100th terms is 100. Diff between 1000th terms?

Diff between 1000th terms will also be A - a.
So difference is 100.
