Arithmetic Progressions (Class 10)

1. The common difference of the AP: 3, 1, -1, -3... is:
(A) 2
(B) -2
(C) 3
(D) 0
2. If the first term 'a' is 10 and common difference 'd' is 10, the first four terms are:
(A) 10, 30, 50, 60
(B) 10, 15, 20, 25
(C) 10, 20, 30, 40
(D) 10, 10, 10, 10
3. The nth term of an AP is given by formula:
(A) 2a + (n-1)d
(B) a + (n-1)d
(C) a + nd
(D) aⁿ - 1
4. Which of the following is NOT an AP?
(A) 2, 4, 6, 8
(B) 5, 10, 15, 20
(C) 1, 2, 4, 8
(D) 3, 3, 3, 3
5. The 10th term of the AP: 5, 8, 11, 14... is:

(B) 35
(C) 38
(D) 185

(B) 11
(C) 12
(D) 13

(C) 65
(D) 75

(A) 97
(B) 77
(C) -77
(D) -87
10. The sum of first n positive integers is given by:
(A) \(\frac{n}{2}\)
(B) n²
(C) n(n+1)/2
