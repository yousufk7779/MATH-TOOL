1. In ∆ABC, DE || BC. If AD = 1.5 cm, BD = 3 cm and AE = 1 cm, then EC is:
(A) 1.5 cm (B) 3 cm (C) 2 cm (D) 4 cm
Correct Answer: (C)
2. If ∆ABC ~ ∆DEF such that ∠A = 47° and ∠E = 83°, then ∠C is equal to:
(A) 50° (B) 60° (C) 70° (D) 80°
Correct Answer: (A)
3. In ∆ABC, DE || BC. If AD = x, DB = x-2, AE = x+2 and EC = x-1, then value of x is:
(A) 3 (B) 4 (C) 5 (D) 3.5
Correct Answer: (B)
4. If the ratio of the perimeters of two similar triangles is 4:25, then the ratio of the areas of the similar triangles is:
(A) 16:625 (B) 2:5 (C) 4:25 (D) 8:125
Correct Answer: (A)
5. If in two triangles ABC and DEF, \(\frac{AB}{DE}\) = \(\frac{BC}{FD}\), then they will be similar when:
(A) ∠B = ∠E (B) ∠A = ∠D (C) ∠B = ∠D (D) ∠A = ∠F
Correct Answer: (C)
6. A vertical stick 20m long casts a shadow 10m long on the ground. At the same time, a tower casts a shadow 50m long. Calculate the height of the tower.
(A) 100 m (B) 120 m (C) 25 m (D) 200 m
Correct Answer: (A)
7. The diagonals of a rhombus are 16 cm and 12 cm. Then the length of the side of the rhombus is:
(A) 9 cm (B) 10 cm (C) 8 cm (D) 20 cm
Correct Answer: (B)
8. ∆ABC ~ ∆PQR. Area(ABC) = 81 cm², Area(PQR) = 144 cm². If QR = 24 cm, then BC is:
(A) 12 cm (B) 18 cm (C) 15 cm (D) 9 cm
Correct Answer: (B)
9. It is given that ∆ABC ~ ∆EDF such that AB = 5 cm, AC = 7 cm, DF= 15 cm and DE = 12 cm. Then the sum of the remaining sides of the triangles is:
(A) 23.05 cm (B) 16.8 cm (C) 6.25 cm (D) 24 cm
Correct Answer: (A)
10. Two poles of height 6 m and 11 m stand vertically upright on a plane ground. If the distance between their foot is 12 m, then distance between their tops is:
(A) 12 m (B) 14 m (C) 13 m (D) 11 m
Correct Answer: (C)
