1. In which situations does the list of numbers make an AP, and why?
(i) Taxi fare: ₹ 15 for first km, ₹ 8 for additional km.
Fare for 1st km = 15 Fare for 2nd km = 15 + 8 = 23 Fare for 3rd km = 23 + 8 = 31 List: 15, 23, 31, ... Difference = 23 - 15 = 8 Difference = 31 - 23 = 8 Difference is same. Answer: Yes
(ii) Air in cylinder: Removes 14 of remaining air.
Initial air = 1 Removed 1st time = 14 Remaining = 1 - 14 = 34
Removed 2nd time = 14 × 34 = 316 Remaining = 34 - 316 = 12 - 316 = 916
d₁ = 34 - 1 = -14 d₂ = 916 - 34 = -316 Difference is not same. Answer: No
(iii) Well digging cost: ₹ 150 first metre, rises by ₹ 50.
1st metre = 150 2nd metre = 150 + 50 = 200 3rd metre = 200 + 50 = 250 Difference = 50 (constant). Answer: Yes
(iv) Compound interest: ₹ 10000 at 8% per annum.
Amount = P(1 + R100)ⁿ Calculating amounts for each year. Interest is on increasing amount. So difference keeps increasing. Difference is not same. Answer: No
Q2. Write first four terms.
(i) a = 10, d = 10
a₁ = 10 a₂ = 10 + 10 = 20 a₃ = 20 + 10 = 30 a₄ = 30 + 10 = 40 Answer: 10, 20, 30, 40
(ii) a = -2, d = 0
a₁ = -2 a₂ = -2 + 0 = -2 a₃ = -2 a₄ = -2 Answer: -2, -2, -2, -2
(iii) a = 4, d = -3
a₁ = 4 a₂ = 4 - 3 = 1 a₃ = 1 - 3 = -2 a₄ = -2 - 3 = -5 Answer: 4, 1, -2, -5
(iv) a = -1, d = 12
a₁ = -1 a₂ = -1 + 12 = -12 a₃ = -12 + 12 = 0 a₄ = 0 + 12 = 12 Answer: -1, -12, 0, 12
(v) a = -1.25, d = -0.25
a₁ = -1.25 a₂ = -1.25 - 0.25 = -1.50 a₃ = -1.50 - 0.25 = -1.75 a₄ = -1.75 - 0.25 = -2.00 Answer: -1.25, -1.50, -1.75, -2.00
