Chapter 4: Important MCQs
1. Which of the following is a quadratic equation?
(A) x² + 2x + 1 = (4 - x)² + 3 (B) -2x² = (5 - x)(2x - 2/5) (C) (k + 1)x² + 3/2 x = 7, where k = -1 (D) x³ - x² = (x - 1)³
Answer: (D)
2. The roots of the quadratic equation x² - 0.04 = 0 are:
(A) ± 0.2 (B) ± 0.02 (C) 0.4 (D) 2
Answer: (A)
3. If 1/2 is a root of the equation x² + kx - 5/4 = 0, then the value of k is:
(A) 2 (B) -2 (C) 14 (D) 12
Answer: (A)
4. The equation 2x² - √5x + 1 = 0 has:
(A) two distinct real roots (B) two equal real roots (C) no real roots (D) more than 2 roots
Answer: (C)
5. Which constant must be added and subtracted to solve the equation 9x² + 3/4 x - √2 = 0 by the method of completing the square?
(A) 18 (B) 164 (C) 14 (D) 964
Answer: (B)
6. The roots of the quadratic equation 4x² + 12x + 9 = 0 are:
(A) Real and distinct (B) Real and equal (C) No real roots (D) Imaginary
Answer: (B)
7. Values of k for which the quadratic equation 2x² - kx + k = 0 has equal roots is:
(A) 0 only (B) 4 only (C) 8 only (D) 0, 8
Answer: (D)
8. The roots of the equation 7x² + x - 1 = 0 are:
(A) real and distinct (B) real and equal (C) not real (D) none of these
Answer: (A)
9. If the equation x² + 4x + k = 0 has real and distinct roots, then:
(A) k < 4 (B) k > 4 (C) k ≥ 4 (D) k ≤ 4
Answer: (A)
10. The sum of the squares of two consecutive natural numbers is 313. The numbers are:
(A) 12, 13 (B) 13, 14 (C) 11, 12 (D) 14, 15
Answer: (A)
