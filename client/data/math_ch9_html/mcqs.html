Chapter 9: Important MCQs
1. The angle of elevation of the top of a tower from a point on the ground, which is 30 m away from the foot of the tower, is 30°. The height of the tower is:
(A) 10√3 m (B) 10 m (C) 30√3 m (D) 10√2 m
Correct Option: (A) 10√3 m
2. A kite is flying at a height of 60 m above the ground. The string attached to the kite is temporarily tied to a point on the ground. The inclination of the string with the ground is 60°. The length of the string is:
(A) 40√3 m (B) 30√3 m (C) 60√3 m (D) 120√3 m
Correct Option: (A) 40√3 m
3. A tower is 100√3 m high. Find the angle of elevation if its top from a point 100 m away from its foot.
(A) 30° (B) 45° (C) 60° (D) 90°
Correct Option: (C) 60°
4. The shadow of a tower is equal to its height at 10:45 AM. The sun's altitude is:
(A) 30° (B) 45° (C) 60° (D) 90°
Correct Option: (B) 45°
5. If the angle of depression of an object from a 75 m high tower is 30°, the distance of the object from the tower is:
(A) 75√3 m (B) 75 m (C) 25√3 m (D) 50 m
Correct Option: (A) 75√3 m
6. A pole 6 m high casts a shadow 2√3 m long on the ground, then the sun's elevation is:
(A) 60° (B) 30° (C) 45° (D) 90°
Correct Option: (A) 60°
7. From a point on the ground, the angles of elevation of the bottom and the top of a transmission tower fixed at the top of a 20 m high building are 45° and 60° respectively. The height of the tower is:
(A) 20(√3 - 1) m (B) 20√3 m (C) 20 m (D) 10√3 m
Correct Option: (A) 20(√3 - 1) m
8. The angle of elevation of the top of a tower from two points distant s and t from its foot are complementary. Then the height of the tower is:
(A) st (B) s²t² (C) √st (D) s/t
Correct Option: (C) √st
9. If the length of the shadow of a tower is increasing, then the angle of elevation of the sun is:
(A) Increasing (B) Decreasing (C) Constant (D) None of these
Correct Option: (B) Decreasing
10. A ladder 15 m long just reaches the top of a vertical wall. If the ladder makes an angle of 60° with the wall, then the height of the wall is:
(A) 15√3 m (B) 15√3⁄2 m (C) 7.5 m (D) 15 m
Correct Option: (C) 7.5 m
